# A 10-Minute Lesson in Psychology: Why Your Side Hustle Might be Draining You

## TL;DR
Having a side hustle isn't just about making more money, but about finding self-worth and a breathing space.

## What is it?
A side hustle refers to having one or multiple part-time jobs or hobbies outside of one's main profession, typically to realize self-worth or increase income. However, having a side hustle is not just about making money; it's also a means of self-realization and growth.

## Why is it important?
In modern society, work pressure and living costs are increasing, and people are seeking new ways to realize their self-worth and increase their income. A side hustle provides a platform for people to pursue their interests and passions while also earning extra income. More importantly, it can give people a sense of accomplishment and pride, making them feel valuable.

## How does it work?
The process of developing a side hustle typically involves the following steps:

```mermaid
graph LR
    A[Discovering Interests] --> B[Learning Skills]
    B --> C[Creating Works]
    C --> D[Sharing Works]
    D --> E[Getting Feedback]
    E --> F[Improving Works]
```

First, people need to discover their interests and passions, then learn relevant skills. Next, they create and share their work with others, receiving feedback and evaluation. Finally, they improve their work and continue learning, achieving self-growth and value.

## Differences from Full-Time Work
The main difference between full-time work and a side hustle is the nature and goals of the work. Full-time work typically aims to achieve economic income and social status, while a side hustle aims to realize self-worth and growth. Full-time work usually requires a significant amount of time and energy, whereas a side hustle can be pursued according to one's interests and schedule.

## In Conclusion
Having a side hustle isn't just about making money; it's about finding self-worth and a breathing space. It provides a platform for people to pursue their interests and passions while also earning extra income. A side hustle is suitable for those seeking self-growth and self-realization, especially those who feel pressured and dissatisfied with their full-time jobs.
