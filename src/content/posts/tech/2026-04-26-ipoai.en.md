## TL;DR
Spatial intelligence is a key technology in artificial intelligence (AI) that enables machines to better understand and interact with the physical world.

## What is Spatial Intelligence?
Spatial intelligence is an AI technology that aims to enable machines to understand and process the environment and objects in physical space. It combines computer vision, machine learning, and logical reasoning to allow machines to perceive, reason, and interact in complex physical environments.

## Why is it Important?
Spatial intelligence helps solve many practical problems, such as robot navigation, object recognition, and scene understanding. It also drives the development of AI technology, particularly in smart manufacturing, smart cities, and autonomous driving.

## How does it Work?
The working principle of spatial intelligence is to perceive and understand the environment and objects in physical space, and then make decisions and interact. This process can be described by the following flowchart:

```mermaid

    A[Perception] -->|Obtain environment and object information| B[Understanding]
    B -->|Establish environment and object models| C[Reasoning]
    C -->|Make decisions and take actions| D[Interaction]
```

## Difference from Traditional AI
The main difference between spatial intelligence and traditional AI is its ability to understand and process physical space. Traditional AI focuses on data and logical reasoning, while spatial intelligence focuses on perceiving and interacting with the physical world.
